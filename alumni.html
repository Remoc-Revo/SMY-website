Alumni - Yala Old Boys Association (YOBA)

Yala Old Boys Association is a social gathering of St. Mary's School Yala's Alumni and was established in 1955 primarily so they can network and exchange ideas. YOBA also offers support to the school in various projects. A few of them have been very supportive and the school encourages others to come back "home" so that together we may take St. Mary's School Yala to greater heights.


Purpose
The business transacted at the annual general meeting of the "YOBA" is:

  1. To elect office bearers for the ensuing year.
  2. To confirm nomination for the general committee for the ensuing year
  3. To appoint the member of the standing committee for the ensuing year
  4. To receive the balance sheets of the "YOBA" for the past year
  5. To elect auditor to the "YOBA" for the ensuing year
  6. To receive the annual reports of the chairman and the secretary
  7. To receive the auditor's report on the financial position.

The annual General Meeting of the "YOBA" is held every year in or about the month of December, and two calendar months notice thereof shall be given to the Hon. Secretary of each duly recognized branch.
