St. Mary's School Yala is one of the esteemed high schools in Kenya.
It was established in 1927, as a mission school by Catholic Fathers, hence a Catholic school. Over the years the school has held a high level of performance in various activities, in addition to the excellent academic performance in the national exams.

The school has produced many productive and successful citizens, who play very important roles in the nation.This is a result of the great morals and enforced by the school.
